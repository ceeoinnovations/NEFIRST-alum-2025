About the Event

Calling all FIRST Robotics alumni! Join us for a day of hands-on innovation and robot hacking! Dive into the exciting new XRP robotics platform, team up with fellow alumni from diverse disciplines, and collaborate to tackle an exhilarating robotics challenge. Whether you’re looking to prototype, learn, or just have a blast, this event is the perfect chance to connect with the vibrant Boston FIRST alumni community, showcase your creativity, and explore cutting-edge robotics. Don’t miss this chance to learn, network, and make robots—and memories!
